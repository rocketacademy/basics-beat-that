Create a 2-digit number by rolling two dice and arranging them to make the highest number possible. The one with the higher number wins!

  1. Player 1, hit the Submit button to roll your numbers.
  2. Then choose how you'd like to arrange the digits by entering 1 or 2.
  3. Player 2, now it's your turn. Hit Submit to roll your numbers.
  4. Then choose how you'd like to arrange the digits.
