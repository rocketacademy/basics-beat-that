How to play beat that❓

Roll two dices and put them in order to make the biggest number possible.

For example, if you roll a 1 and 5, your best answer would be 51.

Your numbers are cumulative, and resets when you refresh the page.

This game is two-player, so grab a buddy! 🙍🏻‍♀️🙍🏽‍♀️
