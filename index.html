Roll dice and create a number combination using the digits rolled.

The player with the higher number wins. To change this, enter "lowest" so that the player with the lower number wins. (Typing "highest will reset the rules.)

By default, 2 dice are rolled. At any time, you may change how many dice to roll by entering a number from 1 to 10.

Player 1, start by hitting Submit to roll your dice.
